Race Timing Services

At Chronometrics LTD, we deliver precise, efficient, and scalable race timing solutions for events ranging from local meets to national competitions. Our team integrates cutting-edge timing systems and hardware to ensure accurate results and seamless athlete tracking.

We specialize in technologies such as RFID and chip timing, providing real-time updates and automated result dissemination. Our setup ensures minimal errors, quick turnaround, and dependable data delivery.

Our systems are used for marathons, track & field, cycling races, triathlons, and more. Whether you're organizing a community event or a high-stakes competition, our team is here to help make it flawless.
